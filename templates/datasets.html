Aerial Detection Datasets

Datasets used for training and evaluation

Why These Datasets?

These datasets provide rotated bounding box annotations required to train and evaluate orientation-aware object detection models.

DOTA Dataset

A large-scale dataset for object detection in aerial images. Contains objects at arbitrary orientations like planes, ships, vehicles and buildings.

Images: 2806
Classes: 15
Annotations: Rotated Bounding Boxes
Domain: Satellite & Drone Imagery
Download Paper

HRSC2016

High resolution ship detection dataset focusing on orientation-aware bounding boxes for maritime surveillance tasks.

Images: 1061
Classes: Ship
Annotations: Rotated Bounding Boxes
Domain: Maritime Monitoring
